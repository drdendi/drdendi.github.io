Pony Parties

Make all your little one’s dreams come true with a Pony Party at Stony Brook Farms. We offer treat bag's for the little ones. A pony party is great fun and makes for a party to remember. Imagine a child’s thrill when they see a pony they can take pictures with and have wonderful and enduring memories that parents and children will cherish! It captures the essence of adventure and childhood. Prices start at $299.
