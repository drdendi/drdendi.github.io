Our Services

Boarding Services

Stony Brook Stables offer show barn level care without the show barn atmosphere. Your horse will be cared for as if it were one of our own; blanket changes, application of fly spray/ sunscreen (owner supplied), scheduling the farm farrier, and a feeding program catered to the needs of your horse are just a few of the many services included in the board rate. Our goal is to keep your horse happy and healthy so you are able to enjoy your time in the barn and in the saddle to the fullest extent!

Amenities include:

  • Direct access to 1,600 + acres of well maintained trails
  • Two Outdoor Arenas
  • New Indoor Arena
  • Ample turn outs rubber coated wire and wood fencing
  • Access to Run-Ins in all turn outs
  • and more!

Please contact us for more details or to arrange a farm visit.

Lesson Program

Stony Brook Stables offers English or Western riding lessons to beginner through intermediate riders. Lessons available for adults and children. Enrolled riders benefit from a hands on approach and will learn to groom and tack their own horse as well as various aspects of horsemanship on the ground; including topics such as wrapping legs, equine nutrition, and first aid care. Our quiet, well schooled lesson horses are patient teachers and suit a variety of riders.

Stony Brook Stables instructors strive to create riders who are strong and secure in the saddle and able to communicate clearly and concisely with their horse with soft, effective aids. The quiet atmosphere of the barn and friendly instructors will make you or your child feel welcome and comfortable to grow as a horse person and most importantly - enjoy it! Beginner to Intermediate Adult lessons offered.

Retirement Boarding

Stony Brook Stables offers both stall and field board for equine retirement at a separate facility located across the street from our public facility. Amenities include an insulated eight stall barn that retains a warm temperature during winter months and always has a cool breeze during summer months. Stalls are 12' x 12', deeply bedded and have heated, automatic water fountains. Two spacious turn out fields offer lush pasture, fresh water that is heated in the winter, and 12' X 24" run ins which are cleaned regularly.

Your retiree will be cared for as one of our own. Blanket changes, fly spray/ sunscreen (provided by owner), a feeding program catered to your horse's needs, and management of farrier and veterinarian appointments are just a few items included in your board. Additionally, Stony Brook can provide grooming and bathing and any extra attention necessary as specified by the owner.

Your senior horse will enjoy a quiet and peaceful retirement with all the spoils they deserve and you will enjoy the peace of mind of knowing your best equine companion is comfortable and well cared for.

Please contact us for availability or with any further questions.

Horses Available for Lease

What does leasing a horse mean?

When you are leasing a horse, you are paying a set fee for the ability and privilege of additional riding time on that particular horse. In many ways, leasing a horse is similar to owning a horse, with fewer financial responsibilities. For many equestrians and horse lovers, leasing a horse is a great step towards eventual horse ownership and learning everything that goes into caring for horses.
