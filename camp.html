Summer Pony Camp 2020

Stony Brook Farm will be offering summer camp July 20th – 24th, 2020 from 8:30 to 3:30. Riders of all skill levels are encouraged to join, however riders must be able to groom and tack their own horses and ride off the lunge line in order to attend. Camp will include two rides a day; a formal group lesson in the morning and a “fun” ride in the afternoon which will include gymkhana type games and bareback riding for those comfortable. Every day will include a ground lesson in the barn, which campers will be invited to practice during their day.

Friday, July 24th – We will invite our riders to show off their skills as we host an on farm gymkhana event from 1-3. This event will include all the games from the fun ride and campers will have the opportunity to polish their horses using the barn skills they’ve acquired over the week. Families are welcome and encouraged to attend.



Daily Camp Schedule Monday - Thursday:

  • 8:30 - 9:00 – Campers arrive, receive horse assignments for the day and begin tacking up
  • 9:00 - 10:00 – Group lesson
  • 10:15 – 11:00 – bath horses, put away
  • 11:15 – 12:00 – ground lesson in the barn
  • 12:15 – 1:00 – lunch
  • 1:00 – 1:30 – tack up
  • 1:30 – 2:30 – Fun ride in ring or trail ride
  • 2:30 – 3:30 – put horses away, practice barn lesson


After care available at additional cost.
