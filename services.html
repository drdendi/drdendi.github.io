Boarding

Stony Brook Farm employees take pride in being friendly, detail oriented, highly motivated horse people. We offer show barn level care without the show barn atmosphere. Your horse will be cared for as if it were one of our own; blanket changes, application of fly spray/ sunscreen (owner supplied), scheduling and holding for the farm farrier, and a feeding program catered to the needs of your horse are just a few of the many services included in the board rate. Our goal is to keep your horse happy and healthy so you are able to enjoy your time in the barn and in the saddle to the fullest extent!

Amenities include:

  • 19 stall barn
  • Direct access to 1,600 + acres of well maintained trails
  • Outdoor Arena with sand footing (indoor coming soon)
  • Ample turn outs with brand new rubber coated wire and wood fencing
  • Access to Run-Ins in all turn outs
  • Cross country jump course
  • Heated water troughs

Stall and field board available. Please contact us for more details or to arrange a farm visit.

Lesson Program

Stony Brook Farm offers English or Western riding lessons to beginner - intermediate riders. Enrolled riders benefit from a hands on approach and will learn to groom and tack their own horse as well as various aspects of horsemanship on the ground; including topics such as wrapping legs, equine nutrition, and first aid care. Our quiet, well schooled lesson horses are patient teachers and suit a variety of riders.

Stony Brook instructors strive to create riders who are strong and secure in the saddle and able to communicate clearly and concisely with their horse with soft, effective aids. The quiet atmosphere of the barn and friendly instructors will make you or your child feel welcome and comfortable to grow as a horse person and most importantly - enjoy it!

Retirement Boarding

Stony Brook Farm offers both stall and field board for equine retirement at a separtate facility located across the street from our public facility. Amenities include an insulated eight stall barn that retains a warm temperature during winter months and always has a cool breeze during summer months. Stalls are 12' x 12', deeply bedded and have heated, automatic water fountains. Two spacious turn out fields offer lush pasture, fresh water that is heated in the winter, and 12' X 24" run ins which are cleaned regularly.

Your retiree will be cared for as one of our own. Blanket changes, fly spray/ sunscreen (provided by owner), a feeding program catered to your horse's needs, and management of farrier and veterinarian appointments are just a few items included in your board. Additionally, Stony Brook can provide grooming and bathing and any extra attention necessary as specified by the owner.

Your senior horse will enjoy a quiet and peaceful retirement with all the spoils they deserve and you will enjoy the peace of mind of knowing your best equine companion is comfortable and well cared for.

Please contact us for availability or with any further questions.
