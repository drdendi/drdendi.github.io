For Sale: Sueño

Sueno is a 15 year old, 14.2 registered Paso Fino. He is a fun and sweet goofball with some brio. While he is a forward going horse, he is also happy to relax, stretch down, and walk on the buckle when asked. He is great on trails – traffic safe, crosses water and bridges, and is not spooky. He has been shown and attended a parade while with us – both of which he behaved beautifully for. He trailers great, stands quietly for everything – grooming, clipping, bathing, and the farrier. Great corto and largo, lovely head carriage and mouth (goes best in a snaffle), and is learning how to canter. UTD on shots, deworming, feet, and dental. Call or text to arrange an appointment to meet him!
