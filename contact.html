Barn Manager: Kate Bosch

Barn manager and instructor Kate Bosch started riding at the age of seven and has passionately pursued a life with horses since then. She graduated Centenary College in 2010 with a Bachelor's Degree in Equine Studies, with her primary focus on barn management. She began working at Stony Brook Farm immediately after graduating from college. Kate prides herself in maintaining a clean, organized barn with a friendly atmosphere. She is a well rounded horse person, with experience in a wide variety of riding disiplines including english, western, gaited horses, and driving. She believes consistency, quietness, and patience play key roles in creating both confident horses and riders.
