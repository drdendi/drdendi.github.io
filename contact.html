Barn Manger/Riding Instructor: Kristina Griffin

Meet Kristina, With a certification in equine management and over two decades of experience in the horse industry, Kristina's got all the horsepower you need! She's ridden in all the styles- English and Western- but her expertise is in hunter jumpers and retraining off the track thoroughbreds. Plus, she's got an exceptional background in customer service, making her equine skills all the more charming. So, let Kristina take the reins and gallop you into the wild and beautiful world of horses!

Visit Kristina on Facebook at Stony Brook Farm, or stop by the barn for questions on boarding and lessons.
